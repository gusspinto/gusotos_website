about the project.

See Website

Hazmat Barbershop is a modern barbershop located in Rio Tinto, Porto. For a variety of customers, the barbershop provides different grooming services such as traditional haircuts, shaves and styles that meet the requirements of each customer.

The primary goal of the project was to develop a website that reflects the barbershop’s unique blend of vintage charm and modern trends. The website was designed with a clean and user-friendly interface, ensuring easy navigation and a well-structured layout that adheres to usability principles. In addition to serving as an online presence for the barbershop, the website also functions as a portfolio showcasing the diverse range of services offered, allowing customers to explore and book appointments with ease.
